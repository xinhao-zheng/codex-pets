# character contract

Both editions depict one character. Rendering may change; identity may not.
两个版本描绘同一角色。渲染可以改变；身份不能。

---

## Identity | 身份

| Feature | Fixed reading |
|---|---|
| Body | plump sea otter; broad round head; small ears; short limbs; thick tapered tail · 丰满海獭、宽圆头、小耳、短肢、粗锥形尾 |
| Face | matte-black rounded nose; cream muzzle; near-black eyes with compact highlights · 哑黑圆鼻、奶油色口鼻、近黑眼与紧凑高光 |
| Palette | dark-brown fur; warm mid-brown face and body; cream muzzle and belly · 深棕毛、暖中棕面身、奶油色口鼻与腹部 |
| Markings | magenta circuits on viewer-left face; cyan circuits on viewer-right · 画面左侧洋红线路、右侧青色线路 |
| Harness | compact charcoal chest rig with restrained cyan and magenta blocks · 紧凑炭黑胸挂与克制青/洋红色块 |
| Tool | short black fishing rod and compact purple reel, attached to both paws · 黑色短钓竿与紫色小线轮，始终与双爪连接 |

Text, logos, HUD panels, and city scenery are reference-image context, not pet
identity. They do not enter a `192 × 208` cell.
文字、标志、HUD 与城市景观属于参考图语境，不属于宠物身份；它们不进入
`192 × 208` 单元。

---

## Asymmetry | 非对称

Viewer-left magenta and viewer-right cyan are semantic marks, not lighting. The
reel and visible tail remain on the approved side of each authored pose family.
A left-facing run is not licensed to swap the character's identity map.
画面左洋红、右青色是语义标记，不是光照。线轮与可见尾巴保持在获准姿态族的既定侧。
向左跑，不构成交换身份地图的许可。

---

## Motion | 动作

The lower body anchors the gaze loop. Eyes and nose lead; head, ears, and upper
torso follow; the fishing rig lags only while its attachment remains legible.
观察循环以下身为锚。眼与鼻先动，头、耳与上身跟随；钓具只在连接仍清楚时产生滞后。

State rows change action, not costume. `running` means active task work rather
than locomotion; `waiting` asks for input; `review` inspects a result. A new prop
would erase the distinction rather than clarify it.
状态行改变动作，不改变服装。`running` 表示任务处理中而非位移，`waiting` 请求输入，
`review` 检查结果。新增道具不会澄清区别，只会抹去区别。

---

## Falsifiers | 证伪条件

Identity fails if circuitry swaps sides, eye spacing or muzzle ratio changes,
the harness or reel changes design, the tail collapses in volume, a prop detaches,
or one row introduces a different costume or device.
若线路换侧、眼距或口鼻比例改变、胸挂或线轮改型、尾部体积坍缩、道具脱离，或任一行
引入另一套服装或设备，身份即告失败。
